A minimalist CSS framework. Why its awesome Milligram provides a minimal setup of styles for a fast and clean starting point. Just it! Only 2kb gzipped! Its not about a UI framework. Specially designed for better performance and higher productivity with fewer properties to reset resulting in cleaner code. Hope you enjoy!
